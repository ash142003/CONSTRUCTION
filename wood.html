Which Wood Should You Use?

Teak Wood

Perfect for luxury interiors and outdoor furniture. Naturally resistant to water, decay, and insects.

Sal Wood

Ideal for load-bearing structures. Dense, durable, and naturally termite-resistant.

Plywood

Flexible and cost-effective for modular furniture, partitions, and interior cabinetry.

MDF

Affordable and smooth-surfaced for painted furniture or design trims in dry areas.
