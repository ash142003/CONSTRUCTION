Which Concrete Should You Use?

Normal Strength Concrete (NSC)

Ideal for residential and light construction, including foundations and non-structural walls.

High Strength Concrete (HSC)

Best for projects needing extra strength like high-rise buildings and bridges.

Lightweight Concrete

Perfect for insulated structures like partition walls and roof slabs.

Self-Compacting Concrete (SCC)

Excellent for decorative and intricate mold designs where vibration is hard to apply.

Fiber Reinforced Concrete (FRC)

Ideal for high-traffic and impact-prone areas like highways and industrial zones.
