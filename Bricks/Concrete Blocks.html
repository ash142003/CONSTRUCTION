Which Material Should You Use?

Red Bricks

Red bricks are commonly used in residential construction for their durability, cost-effectiveness, and ease of use in both walls and partitions. Ideal for smaller buildings and low-rise construction.

Fly Ash Bricks

Fly ash bricks are environmentally friendly and provide better insulation. They are suitable for energy-efficient buildings and are particularly used in commercial and residential buildings where sustainability is a priority.

Hollow Concrete Blocks

Hollow concrete blocks are perfect for larger structures, including industrial and commercial buildings. They provide sound and thermal insulation and are faster to install due to their size.

Solid Concrete Blocks

Solid concrete blocks are used in load-bearing walls, foundations, and structures requiring high strength. They are ideal for large-scale construction projects, including high-rise buildings and bridges.
